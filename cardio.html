What is Cardio?

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, refers to any physical activity that raises your heart rate and improves the efficiency of your heart and lungs. It’s a fundamental component of overall fitness, contributing to better heart health, stamina, and endurance. Incorporating regular cardio into your routine can lower the risk of heart disease, improve lung capacity, and boost energy levels, making it essential for a healthy and active lifestyle.

Jump Rope

A fun and efficient cardio workout that improves coordination and burns calories. Perfect for quick sessions!

Running

A versatile and accessible cardio exercise for all fitness levels, perfect for improving endurance and cardiovascular health.

Cycling

Boost your stamina while enjoying scenic views or high-intensity workouts on a stationary bike.

Aerobics

Get moving with rhythmic exercises that improve flexibility, coordination, and heart health.

Rowing

A low-impact total-body workout that strengthens muscles while improving cardiovascular endurance.

Swimming

Low-impact, full-body cardio that’s gentle on the joints and excellent for muscle toning and relaxation.
